You weren't *enrolled*. You were **selected**. 🎉

Let's build something real. Welcome aboard! 🚀

## 🧭 What is Comebck Pakistan?

A **builder movement**, not a course. We mirror how real product teams work: squads of four, weekly agile sprints, rotating roles, and one shared goal — **launch a real, open-source AI product that real people use.** No coding background required; the new builder is part product thinker, researcher, designer, tester, communicator, **and AI operator**.

| ✅ What it **is** | ❌ What it is **not** |
|---|---|
| A squad-based, project-driven **AI career accelerator** | A coding bootcamp |
| Real product work: research → build → test → launch → demo | A passive video course / certificate factory |
| **Verifiable proof of work** — commits, a shipped product, a case study | A hackathon or a solo tutorial |
| Visibility to mentors, recruiters & founders | An incubator |

> 💚 *AI will not replace serious builders. It will replace passive learners collecting certificates.*

## 🗓️ The 8-week journey

Each week pairs a theme with a hands-on sprint and a **real product-team role you play** — so by Demo Day, you've worn every hat.

| Wk | Theme | The role you play |
|:--:|---|---|
| 1 | Squad onboarding & agile foundations | 🔎 Customer Researcher |
| 2 | Product owner thinking | 🧭 Product Owner |
| 3 | AI-assisted prototyping | 🎨 Designer |
| 4 | AI-assisted building | 🛠️ AI-Assisted Builder |
| 5 | QA, testing & sprint review | 🧪 QA Tester |
| 6 | Launch, adoption & commercial thinking | 📈 Growth Thinker |
| 7 | Documentation & career packaging | 📝 Documentation Owner |
| 8 | **Demo Day & leaderboard** | 🎤 Demo Lead |

🕐 **Time commitment:** ~5–10 hours/week. *Consistency matters more than intensity.*

**The weekly rhythm:** Plan → Build → Test → Review → Document → Demo. *Every week. Repeat. Improve.* 🔁

## 🌍 Build in public, open-source

Every squad ships a **public GitHub repo** + a **live, deployed MVP** with an honest commit history showing *everyone* contributed. That openness is the whole point — it's the proof a recruiter can inspect. All squad repositories are released under the [**MIT License**](https://opensource.org/licenses/MIT). 🪪
